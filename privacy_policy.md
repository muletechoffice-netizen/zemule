# Zelume Privacy Policy
*Last updated: March 11, 2026*

Zelume ("the app") helps users discover and share local experiences. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

## Information We Collect
- **Account data:** Email/phone number, display name, profile photo, and authentication identifiers when you sign up or sign in.
- **Location data:** Precise GPS location (when you grant permission) to show nearby content and improve relevance. You can disable this in your device settings; the app may have reduced functionality.
- **Device info:** Device model, OS version, language, and app version for diagnostics and to improve compatibility.
- **Usage data:** Screens visited, interactions, and crash reports for troubleshooting and improving the experience.
- **Content you provide:** Posts, ratings, photos, and other information you choose to share through the app.

## How We Use Information
- Provide core features (authentication, location-based results, messaging/sharing).
- Personalize content and recommendations.
- Prevent fraud, secure accounts, and enforce our Terms of Use.
- Diagnose and fix bugs, monitor performance, and plan improvements.

## Sharing and Disclosure
- **Service providers:** Cloud hosting, authentication, databases, analytics, crash reporting, and messaging providers that help us operate the app. They only access data needed to perform their services.
- **Legal & safety:** We may share information to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, or protect users and the service.
- **User-directed sharing:** Content you submit (e.g., posts, ratings, shared links) is visible to others according to in-app settings.

## Data Storage and Security
- Data is stored in secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase/PostgreSQL and object storage) with access controls and encryption in transit. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.
- Backups are retained for continuity and disaster recovery.

## Data Retention
- We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account, after which we delete or de-identify associated data unless retention is legally required.

## Your Choices
- **Permissions:** You can revoke location, camera, photos, or notification permissions in your device settings.
- **Access & deletion:** Email us to request a copy of your data or deletion of your account.
- **Marketing:** We currently do not serve in-app ads or sell personal data.

## Children
Zelume is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided information, contact us so we can delete it.

## Contact
For questions or requests, email: privacy@zelume.com
